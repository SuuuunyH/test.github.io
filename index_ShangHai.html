Shanghai is one of the most prosperous cities in the world. In the process of Shanghai becoming so prosperous, there are many places where the city changes beyond your imagination. Let us talk about the changes of Shanghai’s Wujiaochang today.

-

Turning old Chinese ancient architecture into a modern high-tech building

Increase the density of the store, making the business district more comprehensive and more prosperous

Transforming some video shops into a university to provide a good learning atmosphere for the residents of Wujiaochang

Business change

The commercial facilities near Wujiaochang are basically built in recent years, such as the Oriental Commercial Building, the Atlantic Department Store, the Golden Island Building, and the Hudong Financial Building. Wanda Commercial Plaza, Maccott Jinyi Plaza Hotel, and Hualian Festival City Shopping Center are also under construction. In the future, Wujiaochang will be a sub-center of the city integrating the university campus, science and technology park, modern living area and modern service area. The planned Fortune International Plaza is a CBD project integrating business, office and leisure. It is located in the bustling commercial area of Wujiaochang City Sub-center, just across the road from RT-Mart and B&Q, and Shanghai Wanda Commercial Plaza is also attractive. The eyes of many investors, Wal-Mart, Hong Kong New World Department Store, Shanghai No. 1 Food, Paris Spring, Tellus Home Plaza and other well-known brands have now entered.

Education and medical

Surrounded by the Wujiaochang business district in the center of the plate, the life of the business is quite mature and convenient. Due to the plate and surrounding areas, there are many well-known universities such as Fudan University, Tongji University and Shanghai University of Finance and Economics. At the same time, the Wujiao District Hospital is located next to the roundabout. The plate is also very convenient and convenient to Changhai Hospital, Xinhua Hospital and Dongfang Hepatobiliary Hospital. The education level and medical condition of residents have been clearly improved.

Summary

The Wujiaochang are changing with each passing day, and the traffic environment has changed from dirty, chaotic, and poor to clean, homogeneous, and beautiful. The high-rise buildings have risen from the ground, and the quality of life of the residents has been greatly improved.
