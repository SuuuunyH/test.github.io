Urban Transformation

Shen Zhen & Shang Hai 
Haotong Bu & Zhiwei Huang

Definition

Urban transformation refers to major changes and transitions in various fields and aspects of the city. It is a multi-disciplinary, multi-faceted, multi-level, multi-perspective comprehensive transformation. In the past, most Chinese cities have followed an extensive development path characterized by high growth, high consumption, high emissions, and high expansion. Under this extensive development model, China's urban development has experienced many disadvantages such as disorderly and inefficient development, urban-rural regional development imbalance, and social development imbalance. Obviously this is unsustainable.
